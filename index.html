FF: Fun Fishing

The beginning of the story

Once upon a time the girl in the yellow rubber shoes went fishing. Even though it was her first time, in a few minutes caught a small fish. Because it was not big enough for eating - she let it go.

What happened next?

Waiting...

She was trying for 2 hours to catch something, but it was not successful. She was tired, dissapointed and sad.

Finally...

Later on, she caught a big fish and was very happy! When you wait and try again and again - you will get what you want!

The end of the story!
